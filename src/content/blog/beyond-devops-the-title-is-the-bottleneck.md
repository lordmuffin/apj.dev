## The pitch

The DevOps title was a bridge — a way to drag operations into the same room as development without anyone losing face. It worked. And like any good scaffolding, it has outlived its usefulness.

## What replaces it

> Platform engineering isn't a rebrand. It's a different unit of work.

The unit shifts from "pipelines" to "products that other engineers consume." That changes everything downstream — staffing, on-call, success metrics, what a roadmap even looks like.
